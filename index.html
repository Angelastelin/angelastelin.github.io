MEET THE ARTIST

I am Brazilian, born in Bauru in the state of São Paulo. I spent all of my childhood and youth in the alpha global city of São Paulo and holidaying in Mato Grosso do Sul.I grew up in a family linked to music, literature and visual arts, andsoon I started drawing. When the moment came to decide on my career, considering I enjoyed mathematics and geometric drawings, I opted to study Industrial Design at Fundação Armando Álvares Penteado.Came marriage and the children, I put my career on the side but I was never pulled away from my passion for the Arts; fulfilling myself appreciating artwork and artistic manifestations.
I left Brazil in 1994, following my husband to live in Morocco, Turkey and then Sweden. It was in Istanbul that I had the chance to learn to do turkish miniatures, marbling, painting in gouache and watercolour.
Today, I spend my time between the tranquil life in Åmål, Sweden and the buzz of London, England.It was with the births of my grandchildren that the desire to dedicate myself to the Arts returned with a tremendous strength, with an immense desire to express life, creativity, colours and nature!

MY WORK

Fascinated by colours and geometric figures, as well as by nature; my work mixes the abstract with the figurative.After the particular look of my everyday scenes, it is then that the creativity process starts; between pencils, paints, tapes and photographies.
I like the surprise once I start to remove the tapes. The result appears from the game of figures and empty spaces, between the opacity and transparency, between the luminosity of the colours and the darkness.

GALLERY

In nature I find elements so contradictory and marvellous that become paramount material for my work; such as the dispersed variety and the geometry, their strength and fragility, peace and violence, light and shadow.
I see particles coming together and adding up, shaping scenes of a world that is ever changing.
